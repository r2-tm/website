History of Football

Ancient Origins

Football-like games have been played since ancient times. In ancient China, a game called "cuju" was played, which involved kicking a leather ball through a small opening in a net.

Medieval Europe

During the medieval period, various forms of football were played across Europe. These games were often chaotic and had few rules. They were more about team rivalry and less about organized competition.

Modern Era

The modern version of football began to take shape in the 19th century in England. The establishment of standardized rules and the formation of football clubs led to the sport's rapid growth and global popularity.
